Welcome to CybOrg

At San Diego State University, the student-founded CyberRisk Organization is committed to enhancing comprehension within the dynamic realm of Cybersecurity. Our strategic approach includes hosting professional workshops to explain career trajectories through industry expert-led events, facilitating conversations on advancements and emerging technologies, as well as fostering professional networking opportunities. Additionally, a paramount focus is placed on empowering our members with industry-standard certifications and licenses, thereby reinforcing their journey within the field. All in all, CybOrg serves as a platform for professional growth, fostering a corporate mindset, while maintaining persistent curiosity within the technical domain.
